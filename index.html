Batch Transfers

With this feature multiple payments are processed at the same time. This feature is particularly useful for businesses that need to make payments to several recipients simultaneously, such as payroll processing or vendor payments and more. This is time saving compare to making individual transactions.

Mobile money Transfers

Relpay enables you to deposit, withdraw and transfer funds to other users from your mobile money or Airtel money account, making managing your finances simple and accessible.

Secure Online Wallet

RelPay provides a secure and user-friendly online wallet system that allows you to easily manage your digital currency and payment methods. With RelPay's wallet, you can send and receive payments, view transaction history, and access your funds anytime, anywhere

Pay For Utilities
